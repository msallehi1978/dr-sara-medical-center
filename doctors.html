Lead Dentist & Founder DHA Licensed

Dr. Sara Al Mansoori

BDS, MSc Aesthetic Dentistry (UCL, London)

Dr. Sara founded her eponymous clinic in 2012 with a singular vision: to create a dental practice in Dubai where clinical excellence and patient comfort are never compromised. After completing her Bachelor of Dental Surgery with distinction at the University of Jordan, she went on to pursue a prestigious Master's in Aesthetic Dentistry at University College London.

She is a certified Invisalign® provider, a trained Botox practitioner for dental indications, and holds advanced certifications in implantology from the International Team for Implantology (ITI). She regularly presents at regional dental conferences and contributes to the mentorship of the next generation of UAE-based dentists.

Dr. Sara speaks fluent English, Arabic, and conversational French — and believes every patient deserves a thorough explanation of their treatment in the language they feel most comfortable with.

Our Clinical Philosophy

We believe that dentistry works best when it's truly patient-centred. That means listening before treating, explaining every option clearly, and never recommending a procedure that isn't genuinely in your best interest.

We invest heavily in continuing education and the latest technology — from 3D CBCT scanning and digital smile design to laser dentistry — so that our patients always receive treatment at the frontier of modern dental science.

Above all, we treat every patient the way we would want a member of our own family treated. That standard drives every decision we make.
